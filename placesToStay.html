Places to Stay
Justin Trails Resort

Justin Trails Resort has log cabins, cottages and bed and breakfast suites to plan your stay in. Want to bring your furry friend? This resort is pet-friendly! Enjoy their disc golf courses and walking trials to reconnect with nature. They have a full 200 acres of land for you to explore.

Stout's Island Lodge

Stoutâ€™s island lodge is a historic resort built in the early twentieth century. You can either book your room in the historic building or they also have a few cabins available. To make it even better, they have two private islands you can bring your whole family to!

1954 Railroad Caboose

Have you ever thought about staying in a caboose? Now is your chance to do so! This red railroad caboose is a unique stay. You will have the caboose to yourselves which includes a hot tub, fire pit, porch, and lots of outdoor space to explore.

Woodland House

This house is a special hideaway to detach yourself from the outside world. This house is fully surrounded by beautiful trees. The house has a patio with a firepit and a wrap-around deck for you to enjoy the woods wrapped around it.

The Dome

This house is a fully renovated dome shaped home. The dome is right on the water with a great view and the hosts provide kayaks, paddle boards and even a pontoon! It can sleep up to eight guests and has lots of space and areas to entertain people of all ages.
