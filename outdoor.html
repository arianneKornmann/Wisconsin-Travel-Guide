Outdoors
Geneva Lakeshore Path

This beautiful shore path runs approximately 26 miles of in and out wooded areas. Also, along this lakeshore path, you will see many historic estates and homes that feature amazingly well landscaped yards and gardens. This path is perfect for a history buff or someone who enjoys walks by the water!

Meyers Beach Sea Caves

The mainland sea caves of the Apostle Islands National Lakeshore are breathtaking! These caves are accessible by hiking or boat/kayak. The hiking trail is approximately 4.5 miles long with stream crossings and steep slopes. This trail is NOT ideal for new hikers. The hiking trail is available all year long!

Elroy-Sparta State Trail

This state trail remains one of the most popular trails in the country! While on your hike, you can adventure through 3 rock tunnels and 5 small towns. Elroy-Sparta State Trail travels approximately 32.5 miles. This trail is considered a favorite in the Wisconsin bicycling community.

Pewit's Nest Gorge

Pewits nest is a 30- to 40-foot-deep gorge that was formed during the retreat of the last glacier. Along with the main feature, the gorge, are Skillet Creek, shaded cliffs, and a pine forest which makes for a great hike! This trail is approximately only 0.8 miles long. If you are looking for a laid-back hike with lots of sites to see, this could be the trail for you!

Eagle Trail

Eagle trail is a 2-mile loop. This trail may be difficult due to uneven conditions and seasonal ice. Eagle trail includes lots of cool rock formations which contain fossils of life dated back before dinosaurs! This trail is perfect for taking in all the nature around you.
