Food and Dining
The Diplomat

The Diplomat offers American fare in a sharable plate format. They also have a huge variety of cocktails for a fun night out! Join Chef Dane Baldwin and his team for some good memories.

The Barvarian Bierhaus

The Bavarian Bierhaus is Oktoberfest every day! They have 9 different spaces you can check out. They have hosted many weddings and have been known to have the best fish fry around for more than the last 60 years.

L'Etoile

L’Etoile strongly believes in supporting their local farmers and artisan producers. Even through different owners of the restaurant, they have always committed themselves to supporting the newest generation of farmers. After you enjoy your nice lunch at L’Etoile, you can run down to their bakery located on their first floor.

Monty's Blue Plate Diner

Looking for a classic diner? Monty’s is the perfect place for you. Come and enjoy some of the most classic diner foods. All day breakfast, hamburgers, sandwiches, salads and more!

Hagemeister Park

Hagemeister Park is a 9,000 square foot restaurant which doesn’t include the 4,000 square foot outdoor patio space. They have igloos to eat in during the winter along with party rooms to rent our and music/events! This restaurant is the city’s largest riverfront patio. Come check it out!
