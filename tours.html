Tours
Cave of the Mounds

Tours open daily at Cave of the Mounds, rain or shine. Tour through the underground caves and view an array of colorful crystal formations. You can also tour above ground to discover woodlands, multiple gardens, and a bird observation area.

Dells Boat Tours

Hop on a boat and enjoy the scenic views of the Wisconsin dells. They offer multiple different tours including upper dells, lower dells, sunset dinner cruise, jet boat adventures, and ghost boat tours. Some tours include exclusive shore landing walks and explorations.

Door County Tours

Door County tours come with multiple different tours to choose from so you can get the best out of your trip! This includes lighthouse tours, wine tours, bird tours, and more nature tours. Door County tours are locally owned and operated that love to work with locally owned businesses.

Epic Headquarters

Epic is a software company that was founded in a basement in 1979 with 3 part-time employees. They create software to help people get well, help people stay well, and help future generations be healthier. It seems odd to be touring a software companies’ headquarters…but you won’t want to miss out. Epic’s headquarters is straight out of storybook. Tour through their Barn, Ella’s Carousel, Deep Space, Emerald City, Wizards Academy, and Treehouse.

Jurustic Park

Jurustic Park documents the amateur paleontologist, Clyde Wynia. Clyde has created crazy replicas of extinct creatures that were near Marshfield, Wisconsin during the Iron Age. He has large, medium, and small replicas that he has built overtime made of metal, mild steel, and occasionally stainless steel.
